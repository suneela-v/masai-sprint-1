Dark Chocolate :
Dark chocolate, with its notable deep brown color, is the second most popular type of chocolate.
In recent years, dark chocolate has surged in popularity thanks to a number of articles being published about the health benefits.
Dark chocolate's widely-acclaimed health benefits make it a favorite snack among health-conscious consumers.
